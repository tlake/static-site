Description Appearance Image property of Wizards of the Coast LLC. Personality Biography The empyrean Uldrak quested into the Nine Hells to slay Tiamat and win glory for his deity, Surtur, but he was beaten handily by the dragon queen. The empyrean wasn’t slain; instead, Tiamat cursed him for his impudence by transforming him into a spined devil. The Menagerie found him hiding inside the helmet that he once wore — for millennia, Uldrak has dwelled in this spot, forlornly plotting to restore his true form.
