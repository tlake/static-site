Gimble prepares special defensive magic for Finn

When Gimble Became Lawful Evil in Avernus, he new he had to leave the Hells. However, his lingering friendship with Finn compelled him to first make some special castings into his Ioun stone and give it to Finn, hoping it would help keep her safe.

Details

Before retiring for long rest, Gimble will cast the following spells into the Ioun Stone and present it to Finn.

  • Absorb Elements
  • Shield
  • Feather Fall

These are all reaction spells. Considering Finn, I think it would be fun if these spells auto-activated when warranted, surprising her in the process (though still consuming a reaction).

Unconfirmed Bonus
