The Calendar of Harptos

June 3, 2016 - One minute read - 134 words

Information in this article is pulled from Sword Coast Adventurer’s Guide, pages 14-15.

There are thirty days (three tendays, or “rides”) in each month and five annual holidays that fall between months that make up a year of 365 days on the world of Toril. Once every four years, Shieldmeet occurs as a leap day.
