On Traveling

Neverwinter -> Chult Calculations By Sea Distance from Neverwinter to the southern tip of the Tethyr Peninsula, following the coast as a ship would: ≈ 1600 miles. On this map it’s roughly 16 hexes from the southern tip of the Tethyr Peninsula to Port Nyanzaru as a ship would sail, cutting along the west side of the eastern islands south of the peninsula. Each hex is roughly 25 miles, so 16 * 25 = 400 miles.

The Calendar of Harptos

Information in this article is pulled from Sword Coast Adventurer’s Guide, pages 14-15. There are thirty days (three tendays, or “rides”) in each month and five annual holidays that fall between months that make up a year of 365 days on the world of Toril. Once every four years, Shieldmeet occurs as a leap day.
